I am honored to share that I received the **UGA Outstanding Teaching Assistant Award (Spring 2024)** from the School of Computing.

This recognition means a lot to me because teaching is one of the most rewarding parts of my graduate journey.

I am proud to be recognized alongside Sakher Alqaaidi, Rohan Intipalli, Ghadeer Yassin, and Sixiang Zhang. Congratulations to all of them for their excellent work and dedication to students.

I am grateful to the faculty and students at UGA School of Computing for the trust and support.
